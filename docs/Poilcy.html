Section 01

Introduction

Acuff Analytics LLC ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our AI-powered business automation platform, agent services, dashboards, LinkedIn automation tools, and related services (collectively, the "Services").

Please read this policy carefully. By using our Services, you agree to the practices described here. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of our Services.

Section 02

Information We Collect

Information You Provide

We collect information you provide directly when you:

  • Create an account or configure our agent services
  • Enter contact, company, or CRM data into our platform
  • Connect third-party accounts such as LinkedIn or your email
  • Submit queries or interact with our AI agents
  • Contact us for support or provide feedback

Information Collected Automatically

When you use our Services, we may automatically collect:

  • Usage logs including queries submitted, tool calls executed, and agent responses
  • Device and browser information such as IP address and operating system
  • Session duration and interaction patterns with our dashboards
  • Error logs and diagnostic data to improve service reliability

Information from Third Parties

If you connect third-party services, we may receive:

  • LinkedIn profile data and posting activity through LinkedIn OAuth
  • CRM contact records, deal stages, and pipeline data you import
  • Web search results retrieved on your behalf by our web search tool
Section 03

How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

  • Operate, maintain, and improve our AI agent services
  • Process and respond to queries submitted to our agents
  • Generate and publish LinkedIn content on your behalf when authorized
  • Maintain audit logs for compliance, debugging, and performance monitoring
  • Provide customer support and respond to your requests
  • Send operational communications such as service updates and security alerts
  • Detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access
  • Comply with applicable legal obligations

Our commitment: We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We do not use your data to train AI models without your explicit consent.

Section 04

AI Processing & Local Data Handling

Our platform operates in two modes. Your data handling differs significantly depending on which mode you use:

Free / Local
Ollama Mode

Stays on your machine

All AI inference occurs locally. No query data, prompts, or responses are transmitted to Acuff Analytics or any third-party AI provider.

API Required
Anthropic Claude Mode

Processed via Anthropic

Queries are transmitted to Anthropic's servers. Subject to Anthropic's Privacy Policy. We do not store query content beyond local audit logs.

LinkedIn Automation

When you use our LinkedIn scheduler or posting agents, your LinkedIn access token is stored only in your local environment variables and is never transmitted to or stored on Acuff Analytics servers. All LinkedIn API calls are made directly from your machine to LinkedIn's API.

Section 05

How We Share Your Information

We do not sell, rent, or trade your personal information. We may share information only in the following limited circumstances:

Service Providers

We may share data with trusted third-party vendors who assist in operating our Services, such as cloud hosting providers, under strict confidentiality agreements.

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law, court order, or government authority, or to protect the rights, property, or safety of Acuff Analytics, our customers, or the public.

Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction. We will notify you prior to any such transfer.

Section 06

Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our Services and fulfill the purposes described in this policy:

  • Audit log files (JSONL) are stored locally in your environment and are your responsibility to manage and delete
  • CRM data stored in crm_data.json remains on your local system and is not retained by Acuff Analytics
  • Account information is retained for the duration of your subscription plus 90 days following termination
  • You may request deletion of your account data at any time by contacting us
Section 07

Data Security

We implement reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect your information from unauthorized access, loss, misuse, or alteration:

  • No storage of API keys or access tokens on our servers — all credentials remain in your local environment
  • All third-party API communications use HTTPS/TLS encryption in transit
  • Access to any administrative systems is restricted to authorized personnel only

No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. While we strive to protect your information, we cannot guarantee absolute security. You are responsible for maintaining the security of your own API keys, access tokens, and local environment.

Section 08

Your Privacy Rights

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal information:

  • Access — request a copy of the personal information we hold about you
  • Correction — request correction of inaccurate or incomplete information
  • Deletion — request deletion of your personal information, subject to legal obligations
  • Portability — request your data in a structured, machine-readable format
  • Objection — object to certain processing of your personal information
  • Withdrawal of Consent — withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise any of these rights, please contact us at privacy@acuffanalytics.com. We will respond within 30 days.

Section 09

Our Services integrate with or reference third-party platforms including LinkedIn, Anthropic, Ollama, OpenAI, DuckDuckGo, HubSpot, Salesforce, and others. This Privacy Policy does not cover the practices of those third parties. We encourage you to review the privacy policies of any third-party services you use in conjunction with our platform.

Section 10

Children's Privacy

Our Services are not directed to individuals under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us personal information, please contact us immediately and we will promptly delete it.
