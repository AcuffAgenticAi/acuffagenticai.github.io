About

Data clarity with a security-first mindset.

Acuff Analytics LLC blends professional analytics training with hands-on cybersecurity and operations experience to deliver data you can trust.

Acuff Analytics LLC was founded by Christian Acuff to help organizations turn complex data into simple, secure, and insightful reporting. Christian has supported secure systems, improved workflows, and analyzed mission-critical information across healthcare, corporate, nonprofit, and military environments.


With the Google Data Analytics Professional Certificate, Christian focuses on the full analytics lifecycle—from collecting and cleaning data to analysis, visualization, and storytelling with dashboards. Tools include spreadsheets, SQL, Tableau, and R.


A background in cybersecurity and internal audits shapes every engagement. Data is handled with care, access is considered deliberately, and the outcome is always focused on supporting clear decisions.
