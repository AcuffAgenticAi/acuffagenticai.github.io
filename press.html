The Story

AI that acts needs a system that governs

For two decades, AI was a recommendation engine. Humans decided. Now AI decides. Enterprises are deploying autonomous systems faster than they can govern them — and the consequences of ungoverned autonomy are accelerating.

Control Tower is the first Autonomous AI Oversight System (AAOS): a dedicated governance layer that supervises, validates, and governs autonomous AI before it acts.

Key Messages

For media and analysts

"Black Box AI is a liability"

In the agentic era, AI systems that cannot explain their reasoning are an operational and regulatory risk. Control Tower makes the logic visible.

"The oversight layer is the next infrastructure category"

Just as cloud required monitoring, autonomous AI requires oversight. This is not optional — it is infrastructure.

"Simulation before execution — always"

No action reaches production without first being simulated. This is a technical requirement, not a feature.
